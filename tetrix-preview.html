How to Play

Blocks (tetrominoes) fall from the top of the screen. Move and rotate each block to stack it neatly on the floor or on other blocks. Fill a full horizontal row and it clears, earning points. If the blocks stack up to the top of the screen, the game ends.

Controls

  • ←→ Move — Shift the falling block left or right by one column.
  • ↑ Rotate — Turn the block 90° to fit it into the gap you need.
  • ↓ Soft Drop — Hold to make the block fall faster. You earn a small bonus for each row you drop.
  • Space Hard Drop — Instantly drop the block straight to the bottom. Use this once you've picked your spot.
  • P Pause — Pause or resume the game.
  • M Mute — Turn the music on or off.

Scoring

Standard guideline Tetris rules apply. Clearing lines at once scores 40 / 100 / 300 / 1200 points for 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 lines, multiplied by (level + 1). Soft drop adds 1 point per row, hard drop adds 2 points per row. The level rises every 10 lines cleared, and the game speeds up.
